FAQs

How can I search for jobs?

You can explore JobAid’s job board to find the latest openings in the healthcare sector across Australia. Our search feature allows you to filter jobs by location, job type, or keywords, helping you quickly find roles that match your skills and preferences.

How do I apply for a job?

Once you find a role that interests you, click the "Apply" button on the job listing. You’ll be prompted to fill out an online application, which includes submitting your details, an up-to-date resume, and a cover letter.

Do I have to pay to use JobAid’s services?

No. JobAid’s recruitment services are completely free for job seekers—we’re here to help you find the right opportunity at no cost to you.

Will I get a response after submitting my application?

Yes! As soon as you apply, you’ll receive an email confirming that we’ve received your application.

How can I stay informed about new job openings?

The best way to stay updated is to register your interest with us. Simply upload your latest resume and cover letter through our online registration form. It’s quick and easy, and once you’re in our system, we’ll contact you whenever a job matches your experience and preferences.

How does JobAid handle my personal information?

Your privacy is our priority. We will only share your details with potential employers if you give us permission. You remain in full control of your personal information at all times. For more information, you can review our Privacy Policy.

I’m having trouble uploading documents. What should I do?

If you’re experiencing issues, the most common cause is an unsupported file format. Please ensure your resume and cover letter are saved as .doc, .docx, .pdf, or .txt files before uploading. If you continue to have difficulties, feel free to reach out to us via our online support form, email, or by calling our office.

Where is JobAid located?

JobAid is based in Dandenong, Victoria, and provides recruitment solutions for healthcare employers across the state. Whether you're looking for work in metropolitan Melbourne or regional areas, we can help connect you with the right opportunities.

What industries does JobAid specialise in?

We focus on Permanent, and Casual staffing solutions within the healthcare sector, including:

  • Aged Care
  • NDIS Support Work
  • Nursing & Allied Health
  • Medical Administration
  • Disability & Community Services
